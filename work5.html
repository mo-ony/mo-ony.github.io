Project Details

Its a platform of the Ajman Center for Social Responsibility (ACSR), which is a government entity in the Emirate of Ajman in the United Arab Emirates.
it's build using Bubble.io
The website contains information about the ACSR and its activities related to promoting social responsibility and sustainability in Ajman.
It contain information about the ACSR's vision, mission, and values, as well as information about its programs, initiatives, and events related to social responsibility and sustainability. There may also be information about the ACSR's partnerships and collaborations with other organizations and stakeholders in the UAE and beyond.

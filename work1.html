Project Details

The project is a "development of an adversary simulation tool for attacks targeted at Windows environment".

Problematic

Recent security research suggests that most companies have unprotected data and poor cybersecurity practices in place, which is due to many reasons ,which are :
- Many businesses can’t afford professional solutions.
- Limited time and human resources to devote to protect against cyber threats.
- Security professionals are always faced with many challenges of different natures.

Benefits of the solution

For System Administrators :
-Low-budget and quick solution.
-Discover weaknesses without relying on professional security teams.
For Red teams :
-Save time and efforts.
-Execute a complete assessment in one click.
For Blue teams :
-Test implemented defense and incident response capabilities.
-Improve the security posture without needing explicit configuration details.

Functionnalities

-Providing a knowledge framework
-Running simulations
-Generating reports.
